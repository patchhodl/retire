Explanation

Assumption: If your Pension is greater than your Desired Income After Tax, any difference will be contributed to your TFSA.

Bank Rate: The rate that banks provide for savings accounts.

Pension Rate: The inflation-adjusted rate that pensions (ex OMERS) will increase at.

CPP/OAS Rate: The inflation-adjusted rate that the Canadian government will increase CPP and OAS at each year.

Portfolio Growth Rate: This is the assumed growth rate for the RRSP, TFSA and LIRA.

Consumer Inflation Rate: Assume you need $100,000 this year and the CIR is 0.05 / 5%; the following year you will need $105,000 to have the same standard of living.
